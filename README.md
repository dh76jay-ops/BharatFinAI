# 🇮🇳 BharatFinAI
### AI-Powered Investment Research & Portfolio Intelligence Platform

BharatFinAI is an AI-driven stock research platform designed to help retail investors, students, and researchers analyze Indian equities using a combination of technical analysis, risk intelligence, portfolio diagnostics, sentiment analysis, and investor-inspired frameworks.

The project aims to bridge the gap between raw market data and actionable insights through explainable AI-driven decision support.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Retail investors often struggle with:

- Information overload
- Lack of risk awareness
- Emotional decision-making
- Difficulty interpreting technical indicators
- Absence of structured portfolio evaluation

Most tools provide data.

BharatFinAI attempts to provide reasoning.
